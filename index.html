Introduction

Welcome to my personal tech journey page! My name is Saviour Eking, and I am thrilled to share my aspirations and goals for the next two years in the tech field. This journey is not just about acquiring new skills but about transforming my passion for technology into a meaningful and impactful career.

The Spark

It all started with a simple curiosity about how websites and applications work. I remember the first time I wrote a line of HTML and saw it come to life in a browser. That small achievement sparked a fire in me, driving me to explore deeper into the realms of programming and development. Over the years, this curiosity has grown into a full-fledged passion.

Goals for the Next Two Years

As I embark on this journey, I have set several goals to guide my progress and keep me motivated:

  • Work on my socials and branding: Building a strong online presence and personal brand is crucial in today's tech landscape. I plan to actively engage on platforms like LinkedIn, Twitter, and GitHub, sharing my projects, insights, and learning experiences. Additionally, I'll develop a consistent personal brand that reflects my professional values and skills, creating a portfolio website to showcase my work and achievements.
  • Learn and Master React: React is a powerful library that has revolutionized front-end development. Over the next year, I aim to master React, understanding its core principles, best practices, and advanced concepts.
  • Build and Deploy Full-Stack Applications: Building complete applications from scratch is a goal I am excited about. I plan to create at least three full-stack projects, incorporating various technologies and deploying them for real-world use.
  • Contribute to Open-Source Projects: The open-source community is an incredible place to learn and grow. I aim to contribute to meaningful projects, collaborating with other developers and giving back to the community that has given me so much.
  • Secure a Front-End Developer Position: My ultimate goal is to secure a position as a front-end developer at a forward-thinking company. I am committed to honing my skills and building a portfolio that showcases my capabilities and passion.
  • Enhance Problem-Solving Skills: Algorithms and data structures are the backbone of efficient programming. I will dedicate time to practicing these concepts, improving my problem-solving skills, and preparing for technical interviews.

Timeline

To achieve these goals, I have laid out a timeline to keep myself on track and measure my progress:

  • Q3 2024: Complete an advanced React course, building a solid foundation in modern front-end development.
  • Q4 2024: Build and launch a personal portfolio website, showcasing my projects and skills.
  • Q1 2025: Contribute to at least two open-source projects, gaining experience and expanding my network.
  • Q2 2025: Apply for (front-end, back-end, full-stack) developer positions, leveraging my portfolio and open-source contributions.
  • Q3 2025 upwards: Continue learning, building projects, and growing professionally in the tech field.

Conclusion

TO ensure I follow through with this goals, I have signed up for HNG Tech Internship and it's been an awesome journey so far! The road ahead is filled with challenges and opportunities, but I am ready to embrace them all. My passion for technology, combined with a clear set of goals and a solid plan, will guide me through this journey. Thank you for taking the time to read about my aspirations. I am excited to see where this path leads and to share my progress along the way.
